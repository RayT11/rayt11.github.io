Trykit - Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store data when you use Trykit's services. (Minkbot and Trykit Scarps)

2. Data Collection

  • 2.1. Chat Storage - By using Minkbot, you give permission for us to store the chats and conversations that occur between you and the bot. This data may be stored to improve the quality and accuracy of our services, troubleshoot any issues, and enhance user experience.
  • 2.2. Data Usage - The data collected from your interactions with Minkbot provides personalized interactions in chats. Trykit "Scarps" does not collect your data.

3. Data Retention

  • 3.1. Retention Period - The data we collect will be stored for a period necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
  • 3.2. Data Deletion - You can request the deletion of your data at any time by contacting us directly.

4. Security

  • 4.1. Data Protection - We take reasonable steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. User Rights

  • 5.1. Access and Control - You have the right to access the data we have collected about you, as well as the right to request updates, corrections, or deletions to your data.
  • 5.2. Withdrawal of Consent - You can withdraw your consent for us to store your chats at any time. Please note that withdrawal of consent may limit your ability to fully use Trykit's services.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

  • 6.1. Modifications - Trykit reserves the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.
